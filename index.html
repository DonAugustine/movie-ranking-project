The Best Movies According To Justin

My top 3 movies of all time.

Field of Dreams

This is one of the best baseball movies I've ever seen.
Kevin Costner is one of the best actors of my generation.
I used to be a baseball player, so it gives me a sense of nostalgia
when I watch it.

Happy Gilmore

This movie is a comedy about golf. Sometimes I feel like Adam Sandler
plays the same character in all of his movies, but he was truly hilarious
in this.

Donnie Darko

This is a strange movie. Personally, I'm a fan of Jake Gyllenhaal's work.
I was riveted when I first watched this because I wasn't sure exactly what
was going on. Was it something supernatural that was happening? Was the main
character crazy? For that reason, it really drew me in.
